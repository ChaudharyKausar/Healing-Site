About Us

Welcome to my platform, where growth, healing, and empowerment meet.

A Space That Understands You

Sometimes, life becomes overwhelming — even when we don’t show it from outside. At Healing Site, we understand that everyone carries their own story, their own struggles and their own quiet battles. This space was created so you don’t have to face any of it alone. Here, you can speak freely, without fear of being judged, misunderstood, or dismissed. We listen with patience, kindness and genuine care, because your feelings deserve to be heard and held gently.

We believe healing is not just about techniques or sessions—it’s about being truly seen and understood. Whether you’re dealing with stress, emotional pain, confusion, or simply trying to find direction, this space is built for you. A space where you can slow down, breathe, reconnect with yourself, and grow at your own pace. At Healing Site, you’ll find support that feels human, grounded, and compassionate—just like a safe corner you can return to whenever life feels heavy.

What You Might Be Going Through

  • Feeling anxious, restless or overthinking everything.
  • Sadness or heaviness that’s hard to explain to others.
  • Struggling to make decisions or constantly delaying things.
  • Low motivation or difficulty staying focused.
  • Low confidence or self-doubt holding you back.
  • Extreme shyness, finding it hard to speak or interact with people.
  • Trouble managing time or staying organized.
  • Mobile or social media habits affecting your daily life.
  • Stress from work, studies, or personal life.
  • Tension or misunderstandings in relationships.
  • Feeling lonely, lost, or disconnected from yourself.
  • Negative thoughts that repeat and weigh you down.

If this feels familiar, you’re not alone. Healing Site is here to provide a safe space where your feelings are understood, and support is always available — a place where you can pause, breathe, and take small steps toward clarity and balance.

How We Help

At Healing Site, we don’t believe in fixed formulas or rushed solutions. We take time to understand you—your story, your emotions, and what you truly need support with. Every session is shaped around your pace, your comfort, and your goals, creating a space where healing feels natural and safe.

We work with evidence-based therapeutic approaches such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Transactional Analysis (TA), and Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy (REBT), etc. These therapies help you gain clarity, manage emotions, develop healthier thought patterns, and build resilience—so you can face life with greater balance, confidence, and self-awareness.

Founder’s Note – Afreen’s Story

HealingSite was created from years of learning, observing, and understanding people. I began my professional journey in the field of science, working as a Research Fellow and later as an educator. Research taught me to look deeper and understand patterns, while teaching brought me close to people—their stress, self-doubt, academic pressure, career confusion, and emotional struggles that often remained unspoken.

Over time, I met individuals from different backgrounds and phases of life who appeared capable and strong on the outside, yet felt overwhelmed within. Many were carrying stress, anxiety, self-doubt, emotional confusion, or unresolved experiences that influenced their relationships, decisions, and sense of self. Often, what felt like a practical problem was rooted in emotional imbalance or a lack of clarity.

HealingSite was founded to create a thoughtful, safe, and ethical space where mental health and career clarity are addressed together. My aim was never to offer quick solutions, but to support individuals in understanding themselves better, processing their emotions, and making informed decisions at their own pace.

For me, healing is not about changing who you are—it is about understanding yourself, building resilience, and moving forward with clarity, balance, and confidence.

— Afreen Sheikh

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of mental health services do you offer?

We provide therapy for anxiety, depression, PTSD, OCD, ADHD, stress management, marriage counseling, career counseling, and more.

How do I know if I need therapy?

If you’re struggling with emotions, stress, or life challenges that affect your daily life, therapy can help you gain clarity and coping strategies.

Do you offer online counseling?

Yes! We provide both in-person and online counseling to ensure accessibility and comfort.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on individual needs. Some may benefit from short-term therapy, while others may need long-term support.

Do you offer career counseling and psychometric assessments?

Yes! We provide career counseling and guidance with psychometric assessments to help students and professionals choose the right career path based on their skills, interests, and personality.

Can I have a pre-clarity session before starting therapy?

Yes! We offer pre-clarity sessions to help you understand how therapy works before committing to full sessions.
